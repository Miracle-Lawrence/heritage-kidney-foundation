About the Foundation

FEAT Ndugwas Heritage Kidney Foundation was established to support patients who cannot afford life-saving kidney treatments. Though connected to Heritage Kidney and Medical Care, the foundation operates as a support system—allowing the public to contribute and ensure that no patient is left without care due to financial challenges.

Our Impact Focus

We are committed to saving lives through targeted support, awareness, and access to quality kidney care.

Dialysis Support

Financial assistance for patients in urgent need of dialysis treatment.

Transplant Funding

Helping patients access life-saving kidney transplant procedures.

Awareness & Screening

Promoting early detection and prevention of kidney disease.
